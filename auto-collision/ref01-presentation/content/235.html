Gravity Feed Spray Gun
Using this cutaway of a gravity feed spray gun, let's look at how the refinish material moves from the spray gun cup, through the spray gun body, and then becomes atomized outside the air cap.The spray gun trigger has two steps. With the triggering of the first step, the air jets are opened, filling the chambers with compressed air.It is with the second triggering that the refinish material flows into the spray gun body.The material follows the passageway through the spray gun body, past the fluid needle, and out the fluid nozzle.As the material exits the fluid nozzle, the air cap directs the compressed air into the material stream. This causes the refinish material to become atomized and form the spray pattern.
