Masking With Plastic
All edges of the areas to be refinished on this vehicle are masked off. The next step is to apply plastic over the entire vehicle. The plastic is pulled from the box directly over the vehicle, making sure to have enough plastic at both the rear and front. The plastic is unfolded and draped down the side of the vehicle. The plastic is adjusted to make sure it is sitting smooth on the vehicle and centered. The spraybooth is turned on to suck the plastic tight to the vehicle. The plastic is then trimmed along the edges of the masking. Use caution when cutting the plastic as to not damage the vehicle or the masking. Next, the cut edges of the plastic are taped down. This vehicle is now fully masked and ready to be wiped down for refinishing.
