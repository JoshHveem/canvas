Masking For Primer
The repair area on this vehicle is ready to be masked for the application of primer-surfacer. We’ll be using the reverse masking technique. This will help avoid any hard spray lines and give us a soft, invisible edge. We’ll apply the tape to the edge of the repair area as if we’re going to cover it with the paper. The paper is then rolled back over the tape line, creating a rolled, soft edge. Additional masking is applied to protect the vehicle from overspray. Now that all adjacent areas are masked to protect from overspray, the repair is ready for the application of primer-surfacer.
