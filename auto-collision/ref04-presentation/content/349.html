Using Clay
There are clay bars that are designed specifically for use in the detailing process. These bars are designed to remove overspray from vehicles as well other contaminants such rail dust and industrial fallout. The bars are designed to be used with a lubricant such as car wash soap and water. We’ll also be using water to rinse the area clean. The dried overspray on this windshield is a good example of where clay is effective. The clay bar is formed into a pancake shape making sure the wiping surface is clean. We’ll start by wetting the surface and the bar with the recommended soap and water solution. The overspray is then rubbed with the clay bar. You should feel a noticeable difference in wiping resistance of the clay bar as the overspray is removed from the glass. The soap solution is rinsed off with water to check for any additional overspray. Since the overspray is all removed, the glass can now be cleaned with glass cleaner.
