Window Guide (Butterfly) Clips
This is an example of a glass that uses a guide clip, or butterfly clip, to hold the back of the glass into the rear window channel. The raised edges on the back of the butterfly clip snap into the window channel holding the glass in position as it is moved.

To remove the glass from the vehicle, the butterfly clip must be unsnapped or cut from the glass. This requires the installation of a new butterfly clip onto the glass before it is reinstalled in the vehicle.

This pair of specialty pliers will allow the butterfly clip to be snapped together through the hole in the glass, without applying pressure to the glass itself. The opposite end of the pliers is designed to remove a butterfly clip from a glass that is out of the vehicle.
