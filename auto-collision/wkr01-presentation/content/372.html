Cleaning And Disinfecting A Respirator

After extensive use, such as at the end of a workday, take the respirator apart and clean it. Remove the cartridges, and any other removable parts.

Wash all the parts with a mild soap and water solution. Do not use petroleum solvents, which will harm the plastic and rubber parts. Rinse the respirator parts in clear water, removing all traces of soap.

While the respirator is apart, check the different parts for signs of wear. Reassemble the respirator, and seal it in a plastic bag, such as the bag that came with the respirator. Store the respirator in an area that does not expose it to humidity or extreme temperatures.
